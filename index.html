Welcome to RINGStone!


This is a musical website based on the pentatonic scale, which is a musical scale that has five notes.


With the left button of your mouse, click around the left side of the screen to place rings , every ring will resemble a note in the pentatonic scale based on it's COLOR (the higher the ring is on the screen, the higher the note).


Clicking on the Play button, will start the sequencer, which will play notes according to the rings you placed (from left to right).


You can remove rings from the screen by right clicking them or by clicking the Remove all button.


In addition, you can manipulate the sequencer by changing the pentatonic scale, the octave, or the tempo!


Have fun playing with RINGStone!
